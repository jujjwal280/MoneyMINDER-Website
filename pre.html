🚀 Features

Automatic Deduction

Never miss a transaction. Our app intelligently reads bank SMS to log your debits for you.

Interactive Dashboard

Visualize your financial health with dynamic charts and cards that respond to your touch.

Smart Categorization

Our AI automatically sorts transactions into categories like Food, Bills, and Shopping for clear analysis.

Accurate AI Insights

Get highly accurate next-month spending forecasts based on your unique financial history.

Savings Goals

Set financial goals for your next vacation or gadget and track your progress effortlessly.

Custom Reports

Generate weekly, monthly, or custom-date reports to understand spending trends over time.

Customizable Themes

Personalize your experience with beautiful light and dark modes to suit your style.

Bank-Level Security

With secure login and Firebase's robust backend, your financial data is always safe and synced.

Feedback

Share thoughts, suggestions, or report issues directly to the development team.

🧠 AI Model Showcase

Our app adapts to your unique spending behavior using a powerful ensemble of forecasting models, including LSTM, ARIMA, Linear Regression, and Random Forest.

This multi-model approach ensures our predictions are not just smart, but also robust and highly accurate, adapting to both your stable habits and seasonal spending shifts.
